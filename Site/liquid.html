Advantages of Liquid Layout



Liquid layout is often preferred over fixed-width layout for its ability to dynamically resize to whatever the user's resolution may be. This way there is very little wasted space as the screen will always be occupied, and also reduces the need for scrolling down. Liquid layouts also look more fluid and make better use of open space on multiple resolutions and browsers. Many times fixed-width layouts are only optimized for a specific resolution or browser, which often reduces quality in other. Liquid layouts also completely erase the need for scrolling horizontally on smaller screens.
